SYNTHWAVE

About this Genre

This type of sound gained its popularity through the use 8-bit video games
and VHS commercials featuring jingles. Over the years, more video games and
even the Netlifx show Stranger Things featured the Synthwave genre, making its
popularity grow. A genre of music thought to be once lost and buried within the
many other sounds produced is still alive and as alluring as ever.

From the soothing and upbeat tempos to the nostalgia the lyrics bring,
this Synthwave music gives listeners a chance to jump back in time, or
experience a new sound that is sure to leave listeners wanting more.
